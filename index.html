Privacy Policy — Ananda Marga Sangha

Last updated: June 2026

Ananda Marga Sangha (“the App”) is a community application for members of Ananda Marga. This policy describes what information we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account: full name, mobile number, designation (Dada / Didi / Margi), email (optional)
  • Location & address: address lines, pin code, city/locality (from pin code), optional GPS coordinates at registration
  • Profile: family margi details and lesson levels (margi members), center type (Dada/Didi)
  • Activity: events you create or accept, posts, and in-app activity history
  • Device: optional biometric login preference; notification delivery on your device

How we use information

  • Authenticate you and provide community features (nearby members, events, centers, posts)
  • Show your name and locality to other registered members in your city
  • Send in-app notifications about events and community posts
  • Improve and operate the service

Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information you provide is visible to other registered app members as part of community features (e.g. nearby lists, event invites). We do not share data with third-party advertisers.

Storage & security

Data is stored on secure servers. Passwords are stored using one-way hashing. Connections to the server use HTTPS in production/beta builds.

Your choices

  • You can update your profile in the app
  • You can disable biometric login in profile settings
  • To delete your account, contact us (see below)

Children

The App is intended for community members and is not directed at children under 13.
